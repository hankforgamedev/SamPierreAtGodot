# 8th Playtest

strength 
1. 操作變順暢了
2. civil servant mini game, nice


weakness
1. 火車位置歪掉了，沒有在軌道上
2. fontsize hardcoded again (code issue, minor)
3. should have custom keybindings, not just `E` for interaction. like why not `Space` or `Enter`?
	- 推進對話的操作也有點鳥，可以用滑鼠 or `space` but not `E`，應該統一或者都可以
	- should make everything as intuitive and as explicit as possible, at least for the developers (us)
4. perhaps need minimal SFX, otherwise a bit bland
5. WASD (or 上下左右) 不能走斜對角，should have some virtual joystick for player movement control
6. scene train_station crashed when interacted with `>` after showing " 出口在那。天還沒亮。"
7. on second thought, font size and font color might be an issue, as it is the primary concern of playability, at least for now. for instance, the info line below `虎寨城地鐵站` is almost invisible
8. 原文本轉成角色劇本的時候漏掉不少東西，比如這裡「皮耶爾被刺中…」直接跳到「子彈射穿老鼠的肺」。
	- 對話的同時旁邊的角色應該也要跟著動
9. Chapter2 推進對話之後就看不到前面說什麼了。
10. 遊戲劇本寫 "Tiger Fortress City"，README 裡面卻寫 "Huk-Zai City"
11. need playtest mode, 不然只要測 chapter3 的時候得要一路過完 menu, chap1, chap2 
12. `（心裡）` 非常鳥，用顏色或字體區分就好


In short, rough, but shapes start forming and ideas are getting concrete.
